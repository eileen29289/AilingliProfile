7 Thai Restaurants in Columbia

The Thai restaurant mainly serves Thai food, which is usually called Thai cuisine. Traditional Thai cuisine loosely falls into four categories: tom (boiled dishes), yam (spicy salads), tam(pounded foods), and kaeng (curries). Deep-frying, stir-frying and steaming are methods introduced from Chinese cuisine. Thai cooking places emphasis on lightly prepared dishes with strong aromatic components and a spicy edge.
